**CommonGround**

**Research Reading List & Evidence Base**

*v0.1 · March 2026 · Aligned with Guiding Principles v0.2*

This reading list compiles the foundational and recent academic
literature, government documentation, and civil liberties analysis that
grounds each of CommonGround\'s planned cross-layer insights and alert
types. Entries are organized by topic domain and annotated with their
relevance to specific alerts, data sources, and epistemic claim types.
Where the research complicates or qualifies a CommonGround correlation,
that is noted explicitly.

Claim type labels match CommonGround\'s epistemic framework: DOCUMENTED
= primary source evidence of a specific actor or event; MECHANISM =
correlation with a plausible causal pathway supported by research;
CORRELATION = co-occurrence with no established causal mechanism.
FOUNDATIONAL and CRITICAL labels denote key methodological or
complicating sources.

  ----------------- ----------------------------------------------------------
  **DOCUMENTED**    Primary source evidence of a specific actor or event in a
                    specific place and time

  **MECHANISM**     Correlation with a plausible causal pathway supported by
                    external research

  **CORRELATION**   Two things tend to co-occur; causal mechanism not
                    established by the data
  ----------------- ----------------------------------------------------------
